Detailed Description

Persistent data for the actuator-sector rotor model.

Frame convention:

  • CFD/fixed frame: all vectors stored in ActGrid and motion vectors.
  • Rotor local frame: x is radial at zero azimuth, y is clockwise-positive tangential direction at zero azimuth, and z is the rotor normal.
  • Positive omega advances a blade clockwise when viewed along local +z, matching the theory/prototype convention: e_r = (cos(theta), -sin(theta), 0) e_theta = (-sin(theta), -cos(theta), 0)
